How to Install FinTrack-NG

To install this app on your device:

  1. Tap the Share button in your browser (usually at the bottom on iOS or top-right on Android)
  2. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  3. Confirm the installation when prompted
  4. The app will now appear on your home screen like a native app

Note: If you don't see the install option, your browser might not support PWA installation. Try using Chrome, Safari, or Edge.

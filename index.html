Maximize Your Pension Contributions

You can reduce your taxable income by up to 25% through pension contributions. This is one of the most effective ways to lower your tax bill.

Claim All Business Expenses

Keep receipts for all business-related expenses. Office supplies, travel costs, and equipment can all be deducted from your taxable income.

Use Tax-Free Allowances

Every taxpayer is entitled to certain tax-free allowances. Make sure you're claiming all the personal allowances you're entitled to.

Invest in Tax-Free Instruments

Certain investments like government bonds offer tax-free returns. Consider these for part of your investment portfolio.

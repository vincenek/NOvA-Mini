Getting Started

Welcome to OpenLedger Pro! Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Set up your company information in Settings
  2. Add your accounts in the Chart of Accounts
  3. Start recording transactions in the Journal
  4. Create invoices for your clients
  5. Generate reports to view your financial statements

New Features in OpenLedger Pro

  • AI Financial Insights: Get intelligent recommendations based on your financial data
  • Invoice Management: Create, send, and track invoices
  • Automation Rules: Set up rules to automate repetitive tasks
  • Team Collaboration: Invite team members with different access levels
  • Tax Calculation: Automatic tax calculation and reporting

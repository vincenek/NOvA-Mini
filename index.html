DebtFree Compass Tutorial

Welcome to DebtFree Compass!

This quick tutorial will show you how to effectively manage your debts.

Adding Your Loans

Start by adding your loans with the form at the top of the "My Loans" tab.

Include the current balance, interest rate, and minimum payment.

Choosing a Strategy

Select between Avalanche (save on interest) or Snowball (psychological wins) method.

The app will optimize your payoff order based on your choice.

Record payments to track your progress toward becoming debt-free.

How It Works

1

Drag the NukeIt button to your bookmarks bar

This will create a special bookmark that activates the tool on any webpage.

2

Visit any webpage with annoying content

This could be ads, popups, newsletter signups, or anything else you want to remove.

3

Click the NukeIt bookmarklet

This will activate NukeIt mode on the current webpage.

4

Click on any element you want to remove

Each clicked element will disappear with a satisfying animation.

5

Click "Done" when finished

Your changes will be remembered for future visits to the same page.

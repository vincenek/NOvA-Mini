Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a loan?

Go to the Loans tab and fill out the form with your loan details including balance, interest rate, and minimum payment.

What's the difference between avalanche and snowball methods?

Avalanche saves you more money by paying highest interest loans first. Snowball provides psychological wins by paying smallest balances first.

How do I track my progress?

The Dashboard tab shows your overall progress, paid amounts, and savings. You can also set goals in the Goals tab.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI content suggestion work?
Our AI analyzes millions of successful resumes from various industries to provide tailored suggestions for skills, achievements, and professional summaries that resonate with recruiters in your field.
Can I really use this for free?
Yes, our free plan allows you to create a basic resume with one template. For advanced features like AI optimization and multiple templates, we offer paid plans.
How does the job match score work?
Our algorithm compares your resume with job descriptions you provide, analyzing keywords, skills, and experience to show how well you match specific roles and what you can improve.
Will my resume pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)?
Absolutely! All our templates are designed to be ATS-friendly with proper formatting, standard sections, and optimized content that parsing systems can read accurately.

Trading Education

Introduction to Forex Trading
10 min

Forex trading is the exchange of currencies on a decentralized global market. It's the largest financial market in the world, with over $6 trillion traded daily.

Key concepts include currency pairs, pips, lots, and leverage.

Risk Management Strategies
15 min

Proper risk management is crucial for long-term trading success. Never risk more than 1-2% of your account on a single trade.

Use stop-loss orders to limit potential losses and take-profit orders to secure gains.

Technical Analysis Basics
12 min

Technical analysis involves studying price charts to identify patterns and trends.

Common indicators include moving averages, RSI, MACD, and Bollinger Bands.

Welcome to DebtFree Compass!

We'll help you create a personalized debt payoff plan. Our AI-powered system will optimize your payments to save you money and time.

Key Features:

  • Smart payoff strategy optimization
  • Behavioral insights and motivation
  • AI-powered financial recommendations
  • Progress tracking and achievements

Adding Your Loans

Start by adding all your loans in the "My Loans" tab. Be accurate with:

  • Current balances
  • Interest rates
  • Minimum payments

Our system will automatically calculate the optimal payoff strategy.

Choosing Your Strategy

Select between two proven methods:

  • Avalanche: Pay highest interest first (saves money)
  • Snowball: Pay smallest balance first (psychological wins)

Our AI will show you which is better for your situation.

Making Payments & Tracking Progress

Record your payments to:

  • Track your debt-free journey
  • Earn achievements and rewards
  • Get personalized insights
  • Stay motivated with progress visualizations
